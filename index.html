ROCK N' ROLL!

My music taste is quite different compared to most people, as my music taste comes within the realm of rock music. This is the genre of music I am most passionate about, and I would love to tell you why I love it so much.

What defines rock?

  • Rock is defined by how solid its music is, hence the term "Rock." It also is one of the most diverse music genres, with lots of subgenres relating to it. These subgenres even have their own subgenres!

    • SUBGENRES

    1. Metal
    A heavier subgenre, categorized by its distortion, high tempo, and ear-pinching vocals. (e.g. Metallica )
    2. Grunge
    Categorized by its rebellious, low, raw, and dirty sound. (e.g. Nirvana)
    3. Alternative
    Categorized by its unique mixed sound, almost feeling like multiple genres at once. (e.g. Radiohead)
